Who We Are

We’re a team of three undergraduate engineers from NIT Raipur — Tanish Dhavale, Sundram Rai, and Sanskriti Sharma — united by our love for animals and innovation.

What We Do

Dogs can’t speak when something’s wrong. Pawtect is building a smart wearable that tracks real-time health and emotional cues—helping pet parents act before it’s too late.

Why Pawtect?

  • Real-time health monitoring
  • Behavioral alerts and cues
  • Peace of mind when you're away
