About Pawtect

Pawtect is a pet-tech startup born from compassion and innovation. We aim to tackle a fundamental problem — pets can’t speak when something’s wrong. Our smart wearable device empowers pet parents with real-time updates on their pet’s health and emotional state, allowing early detection of discomfort or illness even when you're away.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no pet suffers in silence. With Pawtect, pet parents get peace of mind, early warnings, and personalized data insights to ensure their furry friends stay happy and healthy.

Future Scope

  • AI-based emotion and activity detection
  • Veterinary integrations for early diagnosis
  • Cloud-based health records and analytics
  • Community features for pet-parent collaboration
